Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?

Our return policy allows returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached.

How do I track my order?

You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email. Visit our tracking page for more details.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. Other payment methods may be available depending on your location.

Can I change my shipping address?

Yes, you can change your shipping address before the order is shipped. Please contact our support team for assistance.

How do I cancel my order?

To cancel your order, please contact our support team as soon as possible. Orders that have already been shipped cannot be canceled.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping for an additional fee. You can select the gift wrapping option at checkout.

Are your products eco-friendly?

We are committed to sustainability and many of our products are eco-friendly. Please refer to the product details for specific information.

What is the warranty on your products?

Our products come with a one-year warranty. If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support via email, phone, or live chat. Visit our contact page for more details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. Shipping rates and times vary by location.
